# Research Wiki — Master Index

## Models — Start Here

The wiki's knowledge is organized into **named models** — coherent system definitions
with standards, schemas, and adoption guidance. Each model has a companion standards page.

**[[Model Registry]]** — the entry point for all 15 models with status and standards links.
